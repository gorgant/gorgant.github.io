Section 4: Practical Exercises

Choose a conflict from your past where you felt you could have handled things better. Write a brief analysis of the situation, focusing on your reactions and emotions.

Create a plan for building resilience in the face of future conflicts. Include specific stress management techniques you'll practice, new skills you want to learn, or changes you want to make in your approach to conflict.

Section 5: Communication Checklists

When facing a conflict, follow this checklist to ensure you're applying your resilience strategies:

  • Stay calm and composed.
  • Practice deep breathing or mindfulness.
  • Reflect on past similar conflicts.
  • Identify the main issue and your emotions.
  • Communicate clearly and assertively using "I" statements.
  • Seek solutions collaboratively.
  • Learn from the experience.
