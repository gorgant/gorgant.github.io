Part 6: Communication Checklists

Empathy Checklist:

  • Listen actively without interrupting
  • Ask open-ended questions
  • Reflect back what you hear
  • Validate the other person's feelings
  • Avoid judgment and stay open-minded

Meeting Agenda:

  1. Opening: Brief introduction and purpose of the meeting
  2. Discussion: Encourage open-ended questions and empathetic listening
  3. Resolution: Identify and address the needs and concerns of all parties
  4. Closing: Summarize key points and agree on next steps

Feedback Template:

Introduction: Start with a positive comment or observation
Issue: Clearly describe the issue or concern
Empathy: Acknowledge the person's feelings and perspective
Solution: Suggest a constructive way forward
Closing: End with an encouraging note
