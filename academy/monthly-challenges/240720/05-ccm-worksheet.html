Social Pods Discussion

Discuss the following questions in your social pod:

  • Share your mediation experiences and challenges with the group. What challenges did you face? What techniques were most effective?
  • Compare the effectiveness of different mediation techniques. Which ones helped the most in de-escalating conflict and fostering understanding?
  • How can these techniques be adapted to various conflict situations in your professional and personal life?
  • Reflect on the importance of neutrality and active listening in the role of the mediator. How did these elements impact the mediation process?
  • Analyze a provided case study in your social pod. Discuss how you would apply mediation techniques to resolve the conflict presented.

Case Study: Two team members, Alex and Jordan, have a disagreement over project responsibilities. Alex feels that Jordan is not contributing equally, while Jordan believes that Alex is not communicating expectations clearly. As a mediator, how would you approach this situation using the techniques discussed?
