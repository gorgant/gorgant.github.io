Short Answer Questions

1. Describe a situation in your workplace where a small issue escalated into a larger conflict. What were the initial signs of the conflict?

2. How can fostering a culture of open communication help in preventing conflicts? Provide an example from your own experience.

3. What are the benefits of setting clear expectations within a team? How does this contribute to conflict prevention?

4. Explain the importance of building rapport among team members. How can strong relationships contribute to a harmonious work environment?

5. What steps would you take to address early warning signs of conflict in your team?

Communication Checklist

  • Regularly schedule one-on-one meetings with team members.
  • Encourage open and honest feedback during team meetings.
  • Create an anonymous feedback system for team members to express concerns.
  • Set clear agendas for meetings to ensure all important topics are covered.
